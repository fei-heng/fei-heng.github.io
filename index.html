About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Statistics in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of North Florida. I also serve as the director of The Group for Research and Consulting in Statistics. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Applied Mathematics (Statistics) from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, under the supervision of Yanqing Sun.

Research Interest

Survival and event history analysis, Vaccine clinical trials, Biostatistics, Missing data, Machine learning
